DEM-DNS:
Statistics are stored in "DNS-DEM/rhop_(density)/part_dispersion.txt", where columns correspond to 
- time
- mean displacement in x
- mean displacement in y
- variance in x
- variance in y
- skewness in x
- skewness in y
- kurtosisin x
- kurtosisin y


Upscaled models:
Model statistics are stored in "(model)/rhop_(density)/x/stats.plt", where columns correspond to: 
- time
- mean displacement
- variance
- skewness
- kurtosis
